THE CHOW IS THE PLACE FOR FINE WINE AND DINING.

Fine wine and dining is a specialty here at The Chow. We pride ourselves in the quality of spirits we offer which we gaurantee will lift your spirit. We know you can never go wrong with a tall glass of relaxation.

Our red wine is made from the must (pulp) of red or black grapes and fermentated with grape skins, which gives the wine its color. Our white wine is made by fermenting juice which is made by pressing crushed grapes to extract a juice; the skins are removed and play no further role leaving behind only the delicious, clear result. Every wine bottle is capped with a traditional cork and label ready to be enjoyed by you.

Grab a glass, say a toast, and come indulge in the spirits with us!
